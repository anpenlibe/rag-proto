# Tuition fee/Students' Union fee

*Section: Tuition fee · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/tuition-fee/*

As long as you study, you must pay the prescribed tuition fee/Students' Union fee each semester within the payment period. In the first semester, you complete the admission procedure by paying the fee. In later semesters you thereby re-register for your degree programme.

Choose the applicable question below. You will be forwarded to a new page where you can find the detailed information you need.

## What do students attending the VWU have to keep in mind?

### Info for students in the VWU

If you are currently attending the University Preparation Programme of the Vienna Universities (VWU), you

- first pay the course fee at the VWU and afterwards
- the Students' Union fee at the University of Vienna.

[Detailed info about the payment](/en/admission/non-degree-programme/university-preparation-programme-of-the-vienna-universities-vwu/students-attending-the-vwu/)
