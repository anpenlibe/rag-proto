# Studienberechtigungsprüfung – studying without secondary-school leaving certificate

*Section: Admission · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/studying-without-secondary-school-leaving-certificate/*

The Studienberechtigungsprüfung allows you to study without a secondary-school leaving certificate. One of the prerequisites to be eligible for the Studienberechtigungsprüfung at the University of Vienna is a German language level of at least C1. Therefore, all further information regarding this topic is only available in German.
