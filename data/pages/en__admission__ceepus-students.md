# CEEPUS students

*Section: Admission · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/ceepus-students/*

On this page you can find out which steps you have to complete to be admitted to the degree programme if you have received a letter of award ("Zuerkennungsschreiben").

If you are interested in a CEEPUS mobility grant, please contact the [Central CEEPUS Office](http://www.ceepus.info/content/contact).

## Required documents

- your valid passport or personal ID card (front and back) as JPEG or PNG file
- the CEEPUS Letter of Award and Scholarship Details ("Zuerkennungsschreiben")

## Admission period

- Winter semester 2026/27: from 13 July to 31 October 2026
- Summer semester 2027: from 11 January to 31 March 2027

## Your steps in the admission procedure

### Step 1: Registration in u:space

1. Create your u:account in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en) withing the application period. Make sure to remember your login data. You will need your u:account during your studies.
2. Log into u:space to complete the registration.

### Step 2: Admission

1. Contact the admission office
   Let us know, which programme you were nominated for. Add your Letter of Award and Scholarship Details merged into one PDF file via
   - the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/1945) for bachelor's/diploma programmes or
   - the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/61/create/1995) for master's programmes.
2. You will be preliminarily admitted to the degree programme
   You will receive an e-mail with your student ID number and information on further steps.
3. Pay the Students' Union fee after the appointment
   Once the Students' Union fee is received by the University of Vienna, the admission procedure is complete. You can download your study documents (student record sheet and confirmation of enrolment) in u:space > Personal matters > My documents.

### Step 3: Creating a semester schedule and ordering the u:card

As soon as your tuition fee has been received by the University of Vienna, you can order your student ID card, the [u:card](http://ucard.univie.ac.at/en).

Register for the courses you want to attend. The responsible [StudiesServiceCenter (SSC)/ StudiesServiceUnit (SSS)](https://www.univie.ac.at/ueber-uns/leitung-organisation/studien-lehrorganisation/) will gladly help you with any questions regarding registration for courses and examination.
