# Further information

*Section: Study organisation · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/using-ai-in-your-studies/further-information/*

Further links with additional information, services and guidelines on dealing with artificial intelligence can be found here.

### Who should I ask about AI?

If you have any questions about the use of AI in courses, please always consult the following first:

- the information in u:find
- notes on Moodle and
- information on specific assignments and examinations

If anything is unclear, please always speak to the course teacher.

### Are there any AI courses?

There are interdisciplinary courses on AI that are also eligible for ECTS:

- Extension Curriculum ["Understanding Artificial Intelligence"](https://informatik.univie.ac.at/studium/studienangebot/erweiterungscurricula-ec/kuenstliche-intelligenz-verstehen/)
- Courses on AI in [Studienstart Plus](https://studienstartplus.univie.ac.at/)

### CTL services

The [Centre for Teaching and Learning](https://ctl.univie.ac.at/en/) offers consultations and workshops on AI:

- **[Peer Point](https://ctl.univie.ac.at/angebote-fuer-studierende/student-research-hub/ctl-peer-point/)** for questions about using AI in teaching? Peer Point is here to help you.
- **[Handouts for Students](https://ctl.univie.ac.at/angebote-fuer-studierende/ressourcen/handouts-leitfaeden-und-infomaterial/#c1123913)**
- [Schreib-](https://ctl.univie.ac.at/angebote-fuer-studierende/waehrend-des-studiums/schreibberatung/moegliche-themen-einer-schreibberatung/) und Lernberatungen ([PFSB](https://ctl.univie.ac.at/angebote-fuer-studierende/waehrend-des-studiums/peer-studienfortschrittsberatung/))
- Workshop: "AI, but how? Using tools appropriately in academic work" as part of the writing marathons and the University Library [Nachtschicht](https://bibliothek.univie.ac.at/events/008620.html)

### Library services

Answers to questions about the use of AI in research can be found in the library's services:

- Library courses on AI: How can I use AI tools in my research? What tools are available for literature searches? How can I use such tools effectively? The library offers workshops with different focuses in the series ["AI Literacy - KI-Kompetenz für Studierende"](https://urise.univie.ac.at/course/index.php?categoryid=6&wbtsearch=ai+literacy).
- Library materials for the workshop "AI, but how – effective prompting and research?" [Slides, working materials and further links to the workshop](https://padlet.com/annakrenn4/ai-literacy-ki-kompetenz-f-r-studierende-ai-literacy-ai-comp-tk3fa216fnjk5c22)
- Library materials for the workshop "AI literature search tools" [Slides, working materials and further links to the workshop](https://padlet.com/lillinemetz/ki-literaturrecherche-tools-ai-literacy-2-8ocvkv7s7tn0a5co)
