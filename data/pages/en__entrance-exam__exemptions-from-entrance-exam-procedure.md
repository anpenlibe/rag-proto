# Exemptions from the entrance exam procedure

Find out everything you need to know about possible exemptions from the entrance exam and aptitude test procedure at the University of Vienna on this site.

An exemption from the entrance exam procedure means:

- You don't have to take part in the entrance exam procedure.
- You can apply every semester.
- You don't have to pay an administrative fee.

## Previous degree programme at the University of Vienna

You have started and interrupted a bachelor's or diploma programme with entrance exam/aptitude test procedure at the University of Vienna? If so, you are exempt from the entrance exam procedure for the same programme. This is also the case if there was no entrance exam procedure at the time.

**Follow these steps:**

- Message us via the [contact form](/en/contact) within the [deadline](#c4319).
- Select the topic “Bachelor/diploma programmes”.
- State which programme you would like to resume.
- Attach a scan of your valid passport or personal ID card.

### Attention: Bachelor's programme in Psychology

There are **no exemptions from the entrance exam procedure** in the bachelor's programme in Psychology. Apply for admission within the [application period for degree programmes with entrance exam procedure](/en/deadlines-and-dates) and participate in the written test. Resuming your studies is only possible if you are successful in the entrance exam.

## Changing your degree programme or studying an additional programme within certain groups

An exemption is in place for certain groups of degree programmes: If you are already admitted to a programme at the University of Vienna and want to study an additional programme of or change your programme within that group you do not have to participate in the entrance exam procedure again.

This rule only applies the the University of Vienna's programmes listed below, and not to students of other universities.

## 60 ECTS exemption for the diploma programme in Law

You are exempt from the entrance exam procedure for the diploma programme in Law if you

- were admitted to a jurisprudential degree programme at a recognised educational institution and
- if you can prove having achieved at least 60 ECTS.

The 60 ECTS have to be recognisable for compulsory or elective subjects or compulsory modules of the diploma programme in Law at the University of Vienna. The StudiesServiceCenter Law has created an [overview about recognitions](https://ssc-rechtswissenschaften.univie.ac.at/diplomstudium-neu-2025/anerkennung/) (only available in German). Please check beforehand if your achievements can be recognised.

  If less than 60 ECTS credits can be recognised, no exemption can be made. You will receive the relevant information via e-mail. In this case, you must successfully complete the entrance exam procedure for the diploma programme in Law.

### Active/completed programme in Teacher Education at the University of Vienna

You are exempt from the aptitude test procedure if you

- have interrupted the programme in Teacher Education at the University of Vienna and want to [resume your studies](https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/resumption-of-studies/same-degree-programme/)
- [change your teaching subjects](https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/change-of-degree-programme/change-of-bachelordiploma-programme/#c806587)
- want to be admitted to a [third teaching subject as a complimentary degree programme](https://studieren.univie.ac.at/zulassung/lehramtsstudien/erweiterungsstudium/) (only available in German)
- want to voluntarily transfer to the current curriculum or if are automatically transferred to the current curriculum (e.g. if the curriculum expires).

For resuming your studies or changing your teaching subject message us via the [contact form](/en/contact) during the [application period](#c4319). Send a scan of your passport or personal ID and state the teaching subjects you want to study.

Contact the [StudiesServiceCenter Teacher Education](https://ssc-lehrerinnenbildung.univie.ac.at/) if you have questions regarding the transfer to a new curriculum.

### Active/completed programme in Teacher Education at another educational institution

You are exempt from the aptitude test procedure for the bachelor's programme in Teacher Education at the University of Vienna if you have studied or are currently studying one of the following programmes at a university or college of teacher education in an EU/EEA country or Switzerland.

Sie sind vom Eignungsverfahren für das Bachelorstudium Lehramt an der Universität Wien ausgenommen, wenn Sie eines der folgenden Studien an einer Universität oder Pädagogischen Hochschule eines EU/EWR-Landes oder der Schweiz studieren oder abgeschlossen haben.

- Bachelor's programme in Teacher Education for secondary schools (general education)
- Programme in Business Education
- Programme in Religious Education

**Follow these steps:**

1. Create your u:account in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en). Make sure you remember your login data. You will need the u:account during your studies. Log into u:space and complete the registration.
2. Send the following proofs via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/935)
   1. all pages of your secondary-school leaving certificate merged into one PDF file
      including translation if the document was not issued in German or English
   2. your current confirmation of studies or your degree certificate
3. State which subjects you want to study in your message. Note: If you want to study the teaching subject in Sports and Physical Education please also send your confirmation of studies to [StudiesServiceCenter Sport Science](#).

### Active Teachers

You are exempt from the aptitude test procedure for the bachelor's programme in Teacher Education at the University of Vienna if you fulfil the following:

- You work as teacher at a primary or secondary school with publics rights within the EU/EEA or Switzerland.

**Follow these steps:**

1. Create your u:account in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en). Make sure you remember your login data. You will need the u:account during your studies. Log into u:space and complete the registration.
2. Send the following proofs via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/935)
   1. all pages of your secondary-school leaving certificate/degree certificate merged into one PDF file
      including translation if the document was not issued in German or English
   2. a confirmation about your work issued by the headmaster

### Completed aptitude test procedure at another Austrian university

Once you have successfully completed the aptitude test procedure for the bachelor's programme in Teacher Education for secondary education (general education) at an Austrian university or college for teacher education you are exempt from the aptitude test procedure at the University of Vienna. It does not matter when you completed the aptitude test. The aptitude test procedure for the bachelor's programme in Teacher Education is valid indefinitely.

**Follow these steps:**

1. Create your u:account in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en). Make sure you remember your login data. You will need the u:account during your studies. Log into u:space and complete the registration.
2. Message us via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/935) and state which subjects you want to be admitted to. Send the following proofs (merged into one PDF file) attached to your message:
   - scan of your valid passport or personal ID card (both pages)
   - all pages of your secondary-school leaving
   - confirmation of passing the aptitude test procedure at another university/college for teacher education

### Creative teaching subject at a university of arts

You are exempt from the aptitude test procedure for the bachelor's programme in Teacher Education at the University of Vienna if you study at least one teaching subject at one of the following universities of arts

- [University of Music and Performing Arts Vienna](https://www.mdw.ac.at/studiencenter/zulassung-zum-universitaetsuebergreifenden-lehramts-studium/) (only available in German)
- [Universität of Applied Arts Vienna](https://www.dieangewandte.at/lehramt_kombination) (only available in German)
- [Akademie der bildenden Künste Wien](https://www.akbild.ac.at/de/studium/zulassungspruefungen/bachelorstudium-kuenstlerisches-lehramt) (only available in German)

You first participate in the aptitude test procedure of the university of arts. After you were successful you can be admitted to one or more teaching subjects at the University of Vienna.

If you want to study all teaching subjects at the university of arts you will attend the general educational basics at the University of Vienna as part of a [co-registration](https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/co-registration/).

## Exemptions for exchange students

Exchange students attending the University of Vienna as part of the following mobility programmes are exempt from entrance exams/aptitude test procedure:

- Erasmus+
- Non-EU Student Exchange Programme (formerly Joint Study)

Your point of contact at the University of Vienna is the [International Office](https://international.univie.ac.at/en/).

Students coming to the University of Vienna via CEEPUS programmes: Please contact the [CEEPUS Office](https://oead.at/en/cooperations/international-he-cooperations/ceepus) directly. Here you'll also find further Information about the [admission](https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/ceepus-students/#c1006426) at the University of Vienna.
