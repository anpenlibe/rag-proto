## Your next step

Do you want to expand your professional competencies, open new perspectives for your career, or pursue advanced academic training? The **Postgraduate Center of the University of Vienna** supports you on your educational path with a diverse portfolio of continuing education programs, certificate programs, master’s programs, and openly accessible education formats.

Our programs combine the latest research with practice-orientiented knowledge and stand out for their high quality, interdisciplinary content, and international outlook. This offers you an academic continuing education that purposefully supports your professional and personal development.

The **Campus of the University of Vienna** is your central hub for continuing education. We look forward to welcoming you to the Postgraduate Center – the University of Vienna’s competence center for professional continuing education and lifelong learning.
