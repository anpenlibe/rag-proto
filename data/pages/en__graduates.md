Welcome to the University of Vienna’s alumni community. Now that you have graduated you are entering a new stage of life. This also means that your relationship to your alma mater changes. The University of Vienna would like to continue to accompany you on your path. For this purpose, we are offering the following services to you.

### Be part of the Alumni Community

The University of Vienna [Alumni Community](https://alumni.univie.ac.at/) is a vibrant, open network for all graduates. Here, we connect, exchange experiences, share knowledge – and generate new ideas together.

## Your first steps into professional life

**Advice on getting started on your career.** Many questions arise when it comes to starting a career after graduation. Our advisory team at Uniport, the career service of the University of Vienna, are happy to assist you with personalised consultations and workshops. A number of career events provide insight into different job profiles and the opportunity to get in touch with prospective employers.

***Benefits for alumni:****Members of the Alumni Association receive a 15 % discount on the JobStart seminars offered by the career service Uniport.*

- [Career service of the University of Vienna - UNIPORT](https://www.uniport.at/uniport-fuer-studierende) (in German)
- [Coaching for job applications and CV check](https://www.uniport.at/beratung/coaching) (in German)
- [Current career events](https://www.uniport.at/events/termine) (in German)

### alma Mentoring

#### Finding a mentor

On the [**alma Mentoring Platform**](https://mentoring.univie.ac.at/en/) of the University of Vienna you can find a suitable mentor and benefit from the experiences of other graduates when it comes to starting a career after graduation.

***Benefits for alumni:*** *mentoring free of charge for members of the Alumni Association*

### Job exchange

#### Finding jobs

Your chances of finding a job after graduation increase if you have already collected some work experience during your studies. Uniport, the career service of the University of Vienna, offers its own [**job exchange**](https://www.uniport.at/jobs-praktika/), where internships, trainee programmes as well as jobs in Vienna and all of Austria are advertised.

### Self-employment & establishing a company

#### Incorporation programme

[**u:start**](https://www.alumni.ac.at/portal/berufkarriere/ustart) is the incorporation programme for graduates. It supports academics who are considering self-employment or establishing their own company or enterprise. The training programme assists graduates in developing their initial business idea into a high-quality business plan.

### Keeping up with your discipline

#### Discipline-specific initiatives for alumni

Biologists have different interests than archaeologists or physicists. The [**discipline-specific initiatives for alumni**](https://www.alumni.ac.at/portal/deralumniverband/alumnifachinitiativen) offer the opportunity to keep up with developments in your own discipline even after graduation. ### alma Mentoring

#### Becoming a mentor

No matter if you would like to share your experiences on finding a job, staying abroad or establishing companies and enterprises — on the alma Mentoring Platform of the University of Vienna you can [**become a mentor**](https://mentoring.univie.ac.at/en/page/MentorInnen) and share your knowledge with other graduates.

## Library services

**Continuing to seek knowledge.**Graduating from the University of Vienna does not mean that you can no longer access the University’s information resources. Graduates can continue to use their library accounts or extend them (€ 15 per year) and continue to use electronic resources free of charge (at the locations of the Vienna University Library and via the internal university Wi-Fi).

***Benefits for alumni:****Members of the Alumni Association receive a 50 % discount on library cards (€ 7.50).*

- [Vienna University Library](https://bibliothek.univie.ac.at/en/)
