# AI in your Studies: What is permitted?

*Section: Study organisation · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/using-ai-in-your-studies/ai-in-your-studies-what-is-permitted/*

AI can provide support at various points throughout your degree programme, such as idea structuring, understanding complex content and text revision. However, there are clear guidelines in place regarding the application and extent to which AI can be utilised in degree programmes.

### Position of the University of Vienna

The University of Vienna supports the responsible use of artificial intelligence in degree programmes, provided that such use is transparent, complies with regulations, upholds the [principles of academic integrity](https://backend.univie.ac.at/?id=225768), and that it supports you in achieving the intended learning outcomes.

Whether and how the use of AI is permitted always depends on the specific context. In case of doubt, the following applies: AI is not permitted unless clear permission has been granted.
