## Contact and phone hours

Please use our [contact form](/en/contact-form/). It allows you to be more flexible and to have your request documented in written form.

For reasons of data protection, we cannot provide any information related to personal data on the phone.

You can reach us via telephone by calling +43/1/4277-12100 at the following times:

- Monday and Thursday: from 12:30 p.m. to 01:30 p.m.

Please take note of the working hours of the Admission Office for the timely submission of applications to the Admission Office that are not submitted via u:space. You can find a summary of the working hours [in this proclamation](/fileadmin/user_upload/p_studieren/Studienzulassung/Zulassung/Kundmachung_zur_rechtswirksamen_Einbringung_von_Anbringen.pdf) (in German only).

[## Online info-presentations](/en/admission/online-info-presentations/)

You are interested in studying at the University of Vienna? Come to our info-presentations! In the presentations you will learn everything you need to know for your application to admission to a degree programme. During and after the presentation you can ask your questions in the chat.

It does not matter if you are already in Vienna, or listening from another place: you can comfortably attend the presentation via your own device. Here you can find details regarding [online info-presentation](/en/admission/online-info-presentations/).
