# Resumption of the same degree programme

*Section: Resumption of studies · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/resumption-of-studies/same-degree-programme/*

You were admitted to a regular bachelor/diploma or master programme at the University of Vienna and want to resume studying the same degree programme? Here you can find detailed information about this topic.

## General information about the resumption of studies

- Minimum academic achievement: Since winter semester 2022/23 you have to achieve [at least 16 ECTS](/en/minimum-number-of-credits/) for each bachelor/diploma programme you are admitted to. If this is not the case, the degree programme will be closed. After you have resumed your studies, you can see if you still have to complete the minimum academic achievement in u:space. Make sure to check how many semesters you have left to complete the minimum academic achievement.
- Change of name: If your legal name has changed, please disclose this at an early stage. Message us via the [contact form](/en/contact-form/) and attach a scan of your valid passport or personal ID card and a proof of the name change (e.g. marriage certificate or official notice of the name change).
- Curriculum: You will be admitted to the current curriculum without any exception. If the curriculum has changed, you have to submit an application for admission within the [application period](/en/admission/application-and-admission-periods/) (as you did when you first applied to the degree programme. We therefore recommend: Submit your application for the resumption of studies as early as possible!
- Recognition of exams: The [StudiesServiceCenter (SSC)/StudiesServiceUnit (SSS)](https://www.univie.ac.at/ueber-uns/leitung-organisation/studien-lehrorganisation/) of the respective degree programme can provide information regarding recognition of prior study achievements and help you to organise your studies.
- Completion of resumption: You do not have to book an online appointment.
- Simultaneous admission at two Austrian universities: It is not permitted to be admitted to the same degree programme at two Austrian universities at the same time. If you have already studied the chosen degree programme at another university, please send your confirmation of withdrawal via the [contact form](/en/contact-form/).

### Deadlines

Winter semester 2026/27:

- Application period: from 22 June to 31 October 2026
- Admission period: from 13 July to 31 October 2026
- Semester start: 1 October

Summer semester 2027:

- Application period: from 16 November 2026 to 31 March 2027
- Admission period: from 11 January to 31 March 2027
- Semester start: 1 March

Our tip: Submit your application as early as possible!

## Resuming the same bachelor/diploma programme

### Bachelor programme without entrance exam procedure

You were admitted to a bachelor programme without entrance exam procedure in the past, discontinued your studies and want to resume this degree programme?

Message us via the [contact form](/en/contact-form/) within the application period and attach a scan of your passport or personal ID card. We will implement the resumption of studies within the admission period. Afterwards you will receive information about your further steps via e-mail. The processing may take several days.

You do not have to submit an application for admission in u:space. You do not have to complete any Online-Self-Assessment and you do not need an online appointment.

#### Special conditions for resuming the same bachelor/diploma programme with entrance exam procedure

- Bachelor programme in Psychology
  To resume your studies in the bachelor programme in Psychology, you have to successfully participate in the entrance exam procedure again. Apply for admission in u:space within the [application period for bachelor programmes with entrance exam procedure](/aufnahmeverfahren/fristen-fuer-aufnahme-eignungsverfahren/).
  - [Detailed information about the entrance exam procedure for the bachelor programme in Psychology](/en/entrance-exam/psychology/)
- Bachelor programme in Teacher Education
  You can resume your studies with the same teaching subjects. If you want to change the teaching subject it is a change of degree programme. The same applies for the complementary teaching subject ("Erweiterungsstudium drittes Unterrichtsfach"). For a change of degree programme you have to adhere to a different deadline!
  - [Detailed info about changing a teaching subject](/en/admission/change-of-degree-programme/change-of-bachelordiploma-programme/#c806589)
- Bachelor programme in Sport and Human Movement Science/Teaching subject Sports and Physical Education
  If you have interrupted the bachelor programme in Sport and Human Movement Science or the teaching subject Sports and Physical Education for more than four semesters, you have to retake [sporting aptitude admission test](https://lehre-schmelz.univie.ac.at/zulassung-zum-studium/ergaenzungspruefung/). Please inform yourself regarding this subject at the [SSC Sport Science](https://lehre-schmelz.univie.ac.at/studienservicecenter-und-studienprogrammleitung/). If you have to retake the admission test, you have to apply for admission in u:space within the [application period of the bachelor programme in Sport Science](/en/admission/application-and-admission-periods/#c1068202). The sporting aptitude admission test takes place twice a year.
- Bachelor programme in Transcultural Communication
  According to the current curriculum, the bachelor programme in Transcultural Communication can be completed with two working languages (A and Bx) and extension curricula or three working languages (A, Bx and By). Please disclose how many and which working languages (A, Bx and if applicable By) you choose in your message. You can only choose working languages to which you have been admitted to in the past. It is possible to change the amount of working languages once during your studies. The chosen languages cannot be changed. We recommend a consultation with the [SSC Transcultural Communication](https://transvienna.univie.ac.at/ueber-uns/spl-ssc/), before you resume your studies.

## Resuming the same master programme

### Master programme without selection procedure

You were already admitted to the master programme without selection procedure that you want to resume now but discontinued your studies?

Message us within the application period via the [contact form](/en/contact-form/) and attach a scan of your passport or personal ID card. We will review your request and inform you about your further steps via e-mail.

If the curriculum of your degree programme has not changed, you can resume your studies. You do not have to submit an application for admission in u:space and you do not need an online appointment.

If you discontinued a master programme with selection procedure and want to resume studying this programme you have to [participate in the selection procedure again](/en/admission-procedure/master-programmes/#c876069) and adhere to the respective application period.
