# Changes to exams and teaching

*Section: Accessible studies · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/accessible-studies/changes-to-exams-and-teaching/*

Many students and prospective students with impairments are entitled to request exam adaptations or alternative modes of assessment. Read the information provided below to find out whether you are eligible, how the application and/or implementation works and what types of evidence you have to provide.

## Medical certificate as evidence at the University of Vienna

## Medical certificate as evidence at the University of Vienna

For many adaptations and support offers, you have to provide evidence to the University of Vienna of how the impairment manifests itself and how it affects your studies. Please note the general information about types of evidence you have to present to the University of Vienna.

[Link to further information about evidence of impairment](https://studieren.univie.ac.at/barrierefrei-studieren/anpassungen-bei-pruefungenlven/nachweis-der-beeintraechtigung/)
