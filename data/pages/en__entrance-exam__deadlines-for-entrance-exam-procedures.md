# Deadlines and dates

Are you interested in a bachelor's/diploma programme with an entrance exam/aptitude test procedure? On this page, you will find all the information you need about our application and admission deadlines and test dates for the academic year 2026/27.

## Which period is important for me?

- ### Application period

  The application period is the time frame during which you can apply to study at the University of Vienna by submitting your application for admission.

  During the application period, you can apply for the following: first-time admission, change of programme, admission to an additional programme, and resumption of studies.

  Our tip: Apply right at the beginning of the application period! This will allow you to complete the admission process quickly after the admission period begins.
- ### Admission period

  During the admission period, you complete all the necessary steps in the admission process and are officially admitted.

  Please note: This is only possible if you have applied within the application period and submitted an application for admission to your desired degree programme. You must also meet all admission requirements.

  Our tip: Complete the admission procedure as early as possible. That way, you will get off to a good start in your studies!

### Deadlines for bachelor's/diploma programmes with entrance exam/aptitude test procedure

- #### Application period

  Irrespective of the applicant's citizenship, once per year:

  - **Academc year 2026/27:**
    2 March to 4 May 2026
- #### Admission Period

  Irrespective of the applicant's citizenship

  - **Winter semester 2026/27**
    13 July to 31 October 2026
  - **Summer semester 2027**
    11 January to 31 March 2027

**My desired degree programme says 'Test doesn't take place.' What does that mean?**

There is no written test because there are fewer applications for admission than there are study places available this year.

The application period for the 2026/27 academic year has already passed. You can only be admitted to the course if you

- submitted your application on time and
- meet all the admission requirements
