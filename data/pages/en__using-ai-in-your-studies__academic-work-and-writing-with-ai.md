# Academic work and writing with AI

*Section: Study organisation · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/using-ai-in-your-studies/academic-work-and-writing-with-ai/*

AI can provide valuable support in academic work and writing by generating ideas, structuring texts, revising language and style, organising literature, and clarifying questions of understanding. However, it is important to note that core academic tasks such as developing research questions, constructing arguments, interpreting, and evaluating must be carried out independently.

It is imperative that the use of AI is aligned with the learning outcomes, subjected to critical reflection, and made transparent. Responsibility for content, quality and academic integrity remains with you.
