## Orientation courses

How is my degree programme structured? What are the next steps? How does semester planning and the exams in the introductory and orientation period (STEOP) work? These questions will be answered at the orientation courses (OV) for your degree programme. At many locations, there are additional welcome events alongside the OV. **The programme is constantly being updated**, so do check back from time to time!

[Discover the orientation course!](https://ufind.univie.ac.at/en/search.html?x_c=c%3A24&filter=courses&query=l%3Aov%20l%3Ala-ov%20%2Bct%20c%3A100)

### Guided tours

Does the main building seem like a maze? Don’t worry: after a guided tour, you’ll find your way through the corridors and stairwells with ease.

**Dates and booking:**

Guided tours will take place from September onwards. The booking link and exact dates will be published here as soon as they are available. The meeting point is at the porter’s desk in the auditorium in the main building of the University of Vienna.

Couldn’t get a spot? The [“Uni Wien Guides”](https://event.univie.ac.at/fuehrungen/bereit-fuer-eine-entdeckungsreise/self-guided-tours/app-guides-uni-wien/) app offers a virtual tour with plenty of background information in German and English.
