# Individual study support (ISU)

*Section: Accessible studies · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/accessible-studies/individual-study-support-isu/*

## What is ISU?

ISU is a support service offered by the University of Vienna for students. The following services are currently available:

- [ISU peer counselling](/en/accessible-studies/individual-study-support-isu/isu-peer-counselling/) (counselling in September 2025 at the start of the academic year)
- [ISU learning café](/en/accessible-studies/individual-study-support-isu/the-isu-study-cafe/)

## What is the goal of ISU?

The main goal of individual study support is to enable students to study with (more) equal opportunities. Our services are inclusive. They are also aimed at students with disabilities and at strengthening social interaction and a sense of community among students at the University of Vienna.
