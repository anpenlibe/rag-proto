# The academic year

*Section: Study organisation · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/semester-planning/the-academic-year/*

The academic year is divided into winter semester and summer semester. Each semester consists of a period during which courses are held and a lecuture-free period ("holidays").

- The University of Vienna is closed on [public holidays](https://www.wien.gv.at/amtshelfer/feiertage/).
- [Application and admission periods for degree programmes without an entrance exam procedure](/en/admission/application-and-admission-periods/)
- [Application and admission period for degree programmes with an entrance exam procedure](/en/entrance-exam/deadlines-for-entrance-exam-procedures/)
