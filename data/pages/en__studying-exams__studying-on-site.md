This website provides students with information, tips and tricks as well as with the most important [FAQs on studying on site](/en/studying-exams/studying-on-site/#c612968).

© Barbara Mair

## Check list: Before the beginning of the semester

- Particularly for new students: Get yourself the University of Vienna Welcome Guide as an [e-book](/en/welcome/welcome-guide/) or order a [printed copy](https://www.facultasdruckt.at/welcomeguide) free of charge via facultas.
- Check on [u:find](https://ufind.univie.ac.at/en/index.html), how courses/exams will be held (on site, digital or hybrid).
- Register for all courses/exams on [u:space](https://uspace.univie.ac.at/web/gast/home). Following registration, you can access the learning materials on Moodle.

© Universität Wien / derknopfdruecker.com

## Check list for the day of a scheduled course/exam

- Check your [Webmail account](https://zid.univie.ac.at/en/e-mail) as well as [Moodle](https://moodle.univie.ac.at) to see if there are any short-term changes regarding the relevant course/exam.
- Do not forget to bring
  - laptop, charger and/or a powerbank for courses
  - if necessary a headset with a microphone, if you are at the University and participate in online courses and
  - u:card for exams
