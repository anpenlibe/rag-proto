# Statistical data on master programmes with selection procedure

*Section: Admission · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/master-programmes/statistical-data-on-master-programmes-with-selection-procedure/*

How many people have applied for a master programme with selection procedure, and how many places are available in my preferred degree programme? We believe it is important to provide you with a clear insight into the procedures and general conditions. On this page, you will find up-to-date statistical information on master programmes with selection procedure at the University of Vienna.

## Data for the academic year 2026/27

### Master programme in Psychotherapy

- Registered for the selection procedure: 430
- Study places: 40

The written test takes place on 6 July 2026. The number of participants will be announced thereafter.

### Master programme in Psychology

- Registered for the selection procedure: 409
- Study places: 50

The written test takes place on 7 July 2026. The number of participants will be announced thereafter.

## All other master programmes with selection procedure

For all other master programmes with selection procedure the study places are allocated by selection committees. All applications are currently being reviewed. The applicants will be informed of the outcome by the end of May.
