# u:ai - The AI tool of the University of Vienna

*Section: Study organisation · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/using-ai-in-your-studies/uai-the-university-of-viennas-ai-tool/*

u:ai is the University of Vienna's AI platform, providing students with data protection-compliant access to various AI models. The platform was designed specifically for use in a university context and is part of the Austria-wide Academic AI project.

u:ai can support you in your learning, writing, and analysis—you always retain responsibility for the subject matter and control over the content.

#

## What is u:ai?

u:ai is the University of Vienna's official access point to AI models such as ChatGPT in a secure cloud environment. This means:

- All data is processed within the EU (exception: Internet search, Mistral model)
- The content entered will not be used for training AI models.

However, U:ai was not trained with university-specific content. The AI therefore does not have internal knowledge about courses, exams, or internal institute regulations.

## Access and general conditions

To access u:ai, you need your u:account. Access is via [univie.academic-ai.at](https://univie.academic-ai.at)

You have a monthly credit of €20.00 available for queries. The cost of a specific query depends on the length of the question and the length of the answer (file uploads cost more than text queries, shorter texts less than long texts). You can view and monitor your credit balance directly in u:ai. Once the amount has been used up, you will not be able to use u:ai again until the following month.

Since the monthly credit is limited, u:ai should be used specifically for study and university-related questions. The credit is automatically recharged on the first day of each month. Unused credit cannot be carried over to the following month.

**Knowledge Cut-Off:**

U:ai is currently based on information that was available until October 2024. The system does not know anything that happened after that. To incorporate up-to-date information, you must activate the [Internet search](#c1268815).

## Modules and functions in u:ai

u:ai has a modular structure. Not every module offers the same functions (e.g., uploads or Internet access). Depending on the task, you must select the appropriate module:

- Chatbot: General chat function with image analysis and internet search capabilities
- Image generator: Tool for generating images from text descriptions
- Translator: Translating text and files in the same layout
- Document analysis: Tool for analyzing written content from documents and option for Internet research
- Laboratory: Experimental environment for testing functions and models

### Build your own chatbots with Tailored AI

u:ai enables the creation of so-called tailored AIs. These are individually configured chatbots that permanently store your instructions.

- Such chatbots can optionally work with their own knowledge base (e.g., guidelines, templates), which is then always given priority. This significantly reduces [hallucinations](/en/using-ai-in-your-studies/what-is-artificial-intelligence/#c1268300).
- Once you have built a chatbot, you can also share it with other people.

This means that recurring tasks can be handled more efficiently without having to explain what the AI should do each time.

### Internet search in u:ai

In certain modules, an Internet search can be activated as an option. This accesses external information via the [Perplexity](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perplexity.ai) service. This gives you access to up-to-date information.

### Important note:

When Internet search is activated, the entire chat history to date is used as context. This means that personal or confidential data used in this chat leaves the closed system of u:ai. Data protection compliance is then no longer fully guaranteed.

Therefore, never use personal or confidential content in the same chat as the Internet search, but start a new chat for the Internet search.

## Why should I use u:ai? Other AI tools can do so much more!

When using AI in higher education, it is not only functionality that plays a role, but above all the legal and data protection framework in which a tool is operated. U:ai was developed specifically for the university context and differs in key respects from freely accessible, commercial AI offerings.

### Datenschutz

u:ai is operated in a secure cloud where all data is processed within the EU. The content you enter is not used for training AI models. This means that any text, documents, or questions you enter into u:ai remain in a protected environment.

Bei vielen kommerziellen KI-Tools ist hingegen unklar, wo Daten gespeichert werden, wie lange sie aufbewahrt bleiben und ob sie für Trainingszwecke weiterverwendet werden. Daraus ergibt sich ein erhöhtes Risiko, insbesondere bei der Arbeit mit personenbezogenen, sensiblen oder studienrelevanten Inhalten

What does this mean for me?

- u:ai is compliant with data protection requirements insofar as your personal data and the information you enter are processed in accordance with the data protection standards of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This does not apply if, within the same chat, you use the web search function or the Mistral model.
- When processing the personal data of third parties, the principle of data minimisation applies as a general rule. This means that only the most essential personal data may be collected, processed and stored, in order to achive a specific defined objective.
- In order to process the personal data of third parties or research data, you must obtain the consent of the persons concerned. In this context, please refer to the [information on handling research data](https://ctl.univie.ac.at/fileadmin/user_upload/z_ctl_neu/Angebote_fuer_Studierende/Handouts__Leitfaeden_und_Ressourcen/Handouts_und_Leitfaeden/2024_09_16_AI_and_research_data_EN.pdf).
- **[Sensitive data](https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVGJDSrGw=/?moveToWidget=3458764658613096304&cot=14)** (this is a category of personal data that is particularly sensitive, such as health data) may not be entered into u:ai without the consent of the persons concerned.
- Further information on data protection can be found [here](/en/using-ai-in-your-studies/ai-in-your-studies-what-is-permitted/legal-requirements-data-protection-and-copyright/#c1269046).
- The University of Vienna’s data protection declaration is available at this [link](https://dsba.univie.ac.at/en/data-protection-declaration/).

### Copyright

u:ai also offers an important advantage in terms of copyright. Since uploaded content is not used for AI training, you can upload copyrighted material to u:ai (but please note the regulations on [private copying](/en/using-ai-in-your-studies/ai-in-your-studies-what-is-permitted/legal-requirements-data-protection-and-copyright/#c1268388)).

With commercial tools, uploads can be incorporated into training data or further processed. This means that users lose control over how their content is used. Therefore, you may only use copyrighted material in commercial tools if the data is not used for training AI tools.

In summary, the following applies:

u:ai offers a clearly regulated, transparent, and legally compliant framework for the use of AI in academic studies. This makes the platform particularly suitable for university work processes where data protection, copyright, and scientific integrity play a central role.
