## Questions instead of answers: The Socratic dialogue

A proven method for fostering independence in academic thinking is the Socratic dialogue. In this approach, AI does not simply generate content, but poses targeted questions through which you can reflect on your own arguments, identify weaknesses and develop new perspectives. AI tools act as partners in reflection, for example by presenting different viewpoints and prompting critical reflection.

Instead of relying solely on AI-generated content, you maintain control over the development of your argument while working on research questions, argumentative structures and the interpretation of results with AI support, yet independently.
