# Change of bachelor/diploma programmes

*Section: Admission · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/change-of-degree-programme/change-of-bachelordiploma-programme/*

You would like to change your bachelor/diploma programme?

Your steps in the change of degree programme depend on two factors:

1. Does your chosen degree programme have a compulsory Online-Self-Assessment (OSA) or entrance exam procedure?
2. Is your secondary-school leaving certificate and citizenship from an EU/EEA country or Switzerland, or from a non-EU/EEA country?

## Bachelor programmes with compulsory OSA

### EU/EEA secondary-school leaving certificate and citizenship

1. Complete the [Online-Self-Assessment](https://self-assessment.univie.ac.at/) (OSA) of the desired degree programme. Afterwards you receive your OSA code, which you will need in the further procedure.
2. Apply for admission to the degree programme you would like to change to within the application period. Choose the desired degree programme in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en) > Studies > Apply for admission and upload the [required documents](/en/admission-procedure/bachelordiploma-programmes/). Documents that were already confirmed by the Admission Office (status "confirmed") do not need to be uploaded again. Enter your OSA code.
   Check the status of your application in u:space under "My application". This status has to be "application submitted".
3. Check if all study achievements from the degree programme you want to close are entered in your record of examinations in u:space. Afterwards, message us via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/936) to close this degree programme. The Admission Office will effectuate the change of degree programme, which you will see in u:space > Study overview after a few working days. You will also receive a confirmation via e-mail. Please note: The admission to the new degree programme is only in effect starting from the semester start (1 October/1 March).

### Non-EU/EEA secondary-school leaving certificate

1. Complete the [Online-Self-Assessment](http://self-assessment.univie.ac.at) (OSA) of the desired degree programme. Afterwards you receive your OSA code, which you will need in the further procedure.
2. Apply for admission to the degree programme you would like to change to within the application period. Choose the desired degree programme in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en) > Studies > Apply for admission and upload the [required documents](/en/admission-procedure/bachelordiploma-programmes/). Documents that were already confirmed by the Admission Office (status "confirmed") do not need to be uploaded again. Enter your OSA code.
   Check the status of your application in u:space under "My application". This status has to be "application submitted".
3. You can be admitted to the new degree programme if you fulfil all requirements. You will receive an admission letter to your u:account e-mail address after a processing time of few weeks ([webmail.univie.ac.at](https://www.univie.ac.at/ZID/webmail/index.html?lang=en)). You can also find the admission letter in u:space > Personal matters > My documents.
4. Message us via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/936) after you have received your admission letter. Indicate which degree programme you would like to close. The Admission Office will effectuate the change of degree programme, which you will see in u:space > Study overview after a few working days. You will also receive a confirmation via e-mail.

## Bachelor/diploma programmes with entrance exam procedure

### EU/EEA secondary-school leaving certificate

Entrance exam procedures only take place once per academic year (before the winter semester). The entrance exam procedure for bachelor and diploma programmes is only valid for the winter and summer semester following the procedure. The successfully completed entrance exam procedure for the bachelor programme in Teacher Education it is valid indefinitely.

1. Complete the [Online-Self-Assessment](http://self-assessment.univie.ac.at) (OSA) of the desired degree programme. Afterwards you receive your OSA code, which you will need in the further procedure.
2. Apply for admission to the degree programme you would like to change to within the application period. Choose the desired degree programme in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en) > Studies > Apply for admission and upload the [required documents](/en/entrance-exam/). Documents that were already confirmed by the Admission Office (status "confirmed") do not need to be uploaded again. Enter your OSA code.
   Check the status of your application in u:space under "My application". This status has to be "application submitted". Only then you are registered for the entrance exam procedure.
3. Participate in the [entrance exam](/en/entrance-exam/).
4. An admission to the degree programme is only possible after you have successfully participated in the entrance exam procedure. Message us via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/936) after the results have been announced. Indicate which degree programme you would like to close. The Admission Office will effectuate the change of degree programme, which you will see in u:space > Study overview after a few working days. You will also receive a confirmation via e-mail.

The sporting aptitude admission test takes place twice per year. Therefore, the application for admission to the [bachelor programme in Sport and Human Movement Science](/en/admission/bachelordiploma-programmes/sport-science/) is also possible before the summer semester. Please note the [applicable application and admission deadlines](/en/entrance-exam/deadlines-for-entrance-exam-procedures/#c490977).

### Special cases when changing a bachelor/diploma programme

#### Teacher Education programme: Change of a teaching subject

This kind of change is not possible via u:space. Send us your message in which you indicate the desired change via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/936) during the [deadline](/en/entrance-exam/exemptions-from-entrance-exam-procedure/) (in this case it does not make a difference where your secondary-school leaving certificate is from). The Admission Office will effectuate the change within the admissio period, which you will see after a few days in [u:space](http://uspace.univie.ac.at/en) at Studies > Study overview. You will also receive a confirmation via e-mail. Please note: The admission to the Teacher Education Programme with the new teaching subject is only in effect starting from the semester start (1 October/1 March).

##### Changing to the teaching subject Sports and Physical Education

If you want to change to the teaching subject Sports and Physical Education, you have to register for the bachelor programme Sport and Human Movement Science via u:space and pay 50 Euro for the [sporting aptitude admission test](https://lehre-schmelz.univie.ac.at/zulassung-zum-studium/ergaenzungspruefung/) within the [application period](/en/admission/application-and-admission-periods/#c1068202). For technical reasons, it is not possible to register for the teaching subject Sports and Physical Education via u:space.

If you have successfully participated in the sporting aptitude admission test, send us a message in which you indicate the desired change via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/936). The Admission Office will effectuate the change, which you will see after a few days in u:space at  Studies > Study overview. You will also receive a confirmation via e-mail.

### Note: New curriculum from winter semester 2026/27

- Changing your teaching subjects also constitutes a change of degree programme. This means, from winter semester 2026/27 onwards, changing your teaching subject will automatically result in being transferred to the new curriculum. You do not need to apply separately for the transfer.
- If you study in combination with another university, you also have to notify them of any changes.

[Information about the new curriculum](https://lehramt.univie.ac.at/umstieg-in-das-neue-lehramtsstudium) (only in German)

#### Transcultural Communication: changing the amount of working languages

It is possible to change the number of working languages in the bachelor programme Transcultural Communication once. This means that you can switch from studying with two working languages (A, Bx) and extension curricula to studying with three working languages (A, Bx and By) or vice versa only once.

Send us your message in which you indicate the desired change via the [Servicedesk](https://servicedesk.univie.ac.at/plugins/servlet/desk/portal/62/create/936) during the admission period (in this case it does not make a difference where your secondary-school leaving certificate is from). Specify the number of working languages and which languages (A, Bx and if applicable By) you choose. The Admission Office will effectuate the change, which you will see after a few days in u:space by clicking on Studies > Study overview. You will also receive a confirmation via e-mail. Please note: The admission to the degree programme with the new working languages is only in effect starting from the semester start (1 October/1 March).

## FAQ

/

- My secondary-school leaving certificate is from an EU/EEA country, but I have citizenship of a non-EU/EEA country. How can I change my degree programme

  Submit your application for admission within the application period for non-EU/EEA citizens. Follow the steps for changing your degree programme with secondary-school leaving certificate and citizenship from an EU/EEA country. In this way, your change of degree programme can be conducted earlier and faster – and you can start your new degree programme in a less stressful way.

  You can prove that you belong to a group of persons according to the [Decree on Groups of Persons](/en/non-eueea-nationals-with-equivalent-status/)? If this is the case, you can also submit your application within the application period for EU/EEA citizens. Submit the proof of belonging to a group of persons. The change of degree programme will take longer, as your application must be processed individually, and you have to wait for your admission letter. With this letter you will also receive the info you need to complete the change of degree programme.
