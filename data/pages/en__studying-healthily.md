# Health during your studies

*Section: Study organisation · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/studying-healthily/*

An overview of all services related to balanced nutrition, physical activity and mental health – especially for students. Whether you are looking for a sports course, cafeteria tips or help during stressful periods of study, this website provides information to help you stay healthy, focused and balanced throughout your studies. The range of services will be updated continuously.

## "Health" in the course catalogue

[### The extension curriculum ‘Health literacy and empowerment for students’.](/en/degree-programmes/extension-curricula-and-alternative-extensions/gesundheitskompetenz-und-empowerment-fuer-studierende-ec-15-ects/)

This extension curriculum helps you develop practical skills in health promotion and sustainable nutrition, physical activity and mental health, and supports you in making self-determined, health-related decisions.

[### VU Running as the key to better performance at university](https://ufind.univie.ac.at/en/course.html?lv=330185&semester=2026S)

To deepen evidence-based knowledge of training planning and optimal nutrition, and to gain practical experience together

The course is aimed at both beginners and experienced runners, regardless of their individual level of experience.
