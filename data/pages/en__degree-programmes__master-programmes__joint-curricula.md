# Joint Curricula

*Section: Degree programmes · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/degree-programmes/master-programmes/joint-curricula/*

Joint Programmes are offered in cooperation with one or several other universities. The completion of courses at several universities are an inherent part of the degree programme.

The admission procedure in some cases is not conducted by the University of Vienna and there are special application periods.
