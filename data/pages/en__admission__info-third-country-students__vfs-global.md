# Competence check at VFS Global

*Section: Admission · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/admission/info-third-country-students/vfs-global/*

You have completed your previous education in one of the following countries and are interested in pursuing a bachelor/diploma or master programme at the University of Vienna?

- India
- Pakistan
- Bangladesh

Starting from winter semester 2026/27, you need to take a competence check at VFS Global before submitting an application for admission to a degree programme. You can find all the relevant information on this page.

## Important information about the competence check

The competence check by VFS Global...

- ... takes place **in person and on site** at the VFS Global locations.
- ... **is mandatory** for all prospective students who have completed their previous education in Bangladesh, India or Pakistan and wish to begin a bachelor/diploma or master programme at the University of Vienna in winter semester 2026/27 or thereafter.
- ... **does not replace the formal and content-related review** of your application by the Admission Office.
- **... does not replace** successful participation in the **entrance exam/aptitude test or selection procedure**.
- **... does not replace** completion of **supplementary examinations in the University Preparation Programme of the Vienna Universities** (if any are prescribed in your admission letter).

## Step 1: Inform yourself about the admission requirements

Find out in advance which requirements you must meet with documents from your country in order to fulfil the admission requirements at the University of Vienna. The competence check only makes sense if you meet all the requirements.

Prospective students who do not fulfil all requirements cannot be admitted to a degree programme at the University of Vienna.

Also check the specific requirements for your desired degree programme, such as proof of German and/or English language proficiency and further required documents. You can find information on this on the [page for the respective degree programme](/en/admission-procedure/).

Please also ensure that your documents are correctly legalised and translated.

- [Documents from Bangladesh](#c1270047)
- [Documents from India](#c1270039)
- [Documents from Pakistan](#c1270031)

### Documents from Bangladesh

Your documents have to be legalised by [full diplomatic certification](/en/admission/info-third-country-students/legalisation/#c1121827) and must be translated into either German or English afterwards.

Your steps in the admission procedure depend on the degree programme of your choice.

### Which degree programme are you interested in?

/

- Bachelor/diploma programme

  #### Secondary-school leaving certificate from Bangladesh

  The secondary-school leaving certificate from Bangladesh (Higher Secondary Certificate) is not is not adequate as proof of the general university entrance qualification. The differences to a secondary-school leaving certificate from Austria are too large. Your application for admission will therefore be rejected. This applies regardless of your result in the competence check!

  #### ---

  #### Secondary-school leaving certificate from Bangladesh AND proof of at least two years of studies:

  Your application can be processed if you

  - have a secondary-school leaving certificate from Bangladesh AND
  - have studied for at least two academic years at a recognised university in Bangladesh AND
  - have achieved a CGPA of at least 3.75 (out of 4) in your degree programme.

  Each application must be reviewed individually.

  #### ---

  #### Completion of a bachelor programme of at least four years in Bangladesh:

  If you have already completed a bachelor programme at a recognised university with a stipulated duration of at least four years (full-time), your application can be processed. Each application must be reviewed individually.

  [Detailed information about admission to a bachelor or diploma programme](/en/admission-procedure/bachelordiploma-programmes/)
- Master programme

  #### Bachelor programme of at least four years from Bangladesh:

  Your application can be processed if you

  - have completed a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least four years (full-time) at a recognised university in Section 1 (according to ENIC NARIC AUSTRIA Credential Evaluation) AND
  - have achieved a CGPA of 3.75 (out of 4.0) during your degree programme.

  Each application has to be processed individually.

  If you have a **lower CGPA** OR have completed your degree programme at a **university in Sections 2, 3 and 4**, **you do not meet the requirements** for admission to a master programme at the University of Vienna. Your application will therefore be rejected. This applies regardless of your result in the competence check.

  ---

  #### Completion of a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least four years AND a master programme of two years:

  Your application can be processed if you:

  - have completed a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least four years (full-time) at a recognised university AND
  - have achieved a CGPA of at least 3.75 (out of 4.0) in your bachelor programme AND
  - have completed a master programme with a stipulated duration of at least two years (full-time) at a recognised university AND
  - have achieved a CGPA of at least 3.75 out of 4.0 in your master's programme.

  [Detailed information about admission to master programmes](/en/admission-procedure/master-programmes/)

### Documents from India

Your documents have to be legalised by [apostille](/en/admission/info-third-country-students/legalisation/#c1121828) and must be translated into either German or English afterwards.

Your steps in the admission procedure depend on the degree programme of your choice.

### Which degree programme are you interested in?

/

- Bachelor/diploma programme

  If your secondary-school leaving certificate (12 years of education) is from India and you have achieved at least 85% of the maximum possible marks, your application can be processed. Each application must be reviewed individually.

  [Detailed information about admission to a bachelor or diploma programme](/en/admission-procedure/bachelordiploma-programmes/)
- Master programme

  Your application can be processed if:

  - You have completed a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least three years (full-time) at a recognised university AND
  - your university is classified as Section 1 or 2 (according to ENIC NARIC AUSTRIA Credential Evaluation) AND
  - you have achieved at least 60% of the maximum points available in your studies.

  OR, if:

  - You have completed a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least three years (full-time) at a recognised university AND
  - you have completed your bachelor programme at a state university, which is classified as Section 3 (according to ENIC NARIC AUSTRIA Credential Evaluation) AND
  - you have achieved at least 80% of the maximum points available in your studies.

  Each application must be reviewed individually.

  [Detailed information about admission to master programmes](/en/admission-procedure/master-programmes/)

### Documents from Pakistan

Your documents have to be legalised by [full diplomatic certification](/en/admission/info-third-country-students/legalisation/#c1121827) and must be translated into either German or English afterwards.

Your steps in the admission procedure depend on the degree programme of your choice.

### Which degree programme are you interested in?

/

- Bachelor/diploma programme

  #### Secondary-school leaving certificate from Pakistan:

  If your secondary-school leaving certificate (12 years of education) is from Pakistan and you have achieved at least a result between 990 and 1.100 points, your application can be processed. Each application must be reviewed individually.

  If you have achieved a lower percentage, you do not meet the requirements for admission to a bachelor programme at the University of Vienna. Your application will therefore be rejected. This applies regardless of your result in the competence check.

  ---

  #### Secondary-school leaving certificate from Pakistan AND proof of at least two years of studies:

  Your application can be processed if you:

  - have a secondary-school leaving certificate (12 years of education) from Pakistan AND
  - have studied for at least two academic years at a recognised university in Pakistan AND
  - have achieved a CGPA of at least 3.2 (out of 4.0) in your studies.

  Each application must be reviewed individually.

  ---

  #### Completion of a bachelor programme of at least four years in Pakistan:

  Your application can be processed if you

  - have completed a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least four years (full-time) at a recognised university in Pakistan AND
  - you have achieved a CGPA of at least 3.2 (out of 4.0) in your degree programme.

  Each application must be reviewed individually.

  [Detailed information about admission to a bachelor or diploma programme](/en/admission-procedure/bachelordiploma-programmes/)
- Master programme

  Your application can be processed if you

  - have completed a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least four years (full-time) at a recognised university AND
  - your university is classified as Section 1 (according to ENIC NARIC AUSTRIA Credential Evaluation) AND
  - you have achieved a CGPA of at least 3.5 (out of 4.0) during your degree programme.

  Or, if you:

  - have completed a bachelor programme with a stipulated duration of at least four years (full-time) at a recognised public university (not private!) AND
  - your university is classified as Section 2 (according to ENIC NARIC AUSTRIA Credential Evaluation) AND
  - you have achieved a CGPA of at least 3.5 (out of 4.0) during your degree programme.

  Each application must be reviewed individually.

  If you have a lower CGPA OR have completed your degree programme at a Section 3 or 4 university, you do not meet the requirements for admission to a master programme at the University of Vienna. Your application will therefore be rejected. This applies regardless of your results in the competence check.

  [Detailed information about admission to master programmes](/en/admission-procedure/master-programmes/)

## Step 2: Book an appointment for the competence check

You fulfil the admission requirements? Book an appointment for the competence check at VFS Global and pay the fee of around € 100 directly to VFS Global.

When booking your appointment, you have to indicate which Austrian university you would like to study at and which degree programme you are interested in.

### Appointment

[Book the appointment here](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=LWHGAXGqfEO0Y6oDCSiOO28cS5yMYn1Coq9ZncGMJhxUNDBPSTVOM0xFSUdESVFIRDVXOEFQSE5aUS4u&route=shorturl)

## Step 3: Participate in the competence check

VFS Global will check the following during your personal appointment:

- Your identity (passport)
- Your documents (depending on your chosen degree programme)
- Your general competence in the following areas

- German (for degree programmes instructed in German)
- English
- Mathematics
- Biology
- Chemistry
- History/Geography
- Physics

## Step 4: Submit your application for admission to the degree programme

Follow the steps in the admission procedure, depending on your desired degree programme.

Upload the confirmation of VFS Global in u:space at "Further documents and proofs" as document type "VFS certificate of participation". VFS Global will send the results of the competence check directly to the University of Vienna.

### Don't forget: Application deadlines

Different [application periods](/en/admission/application-and-admission-periods/) apply to bachelor programmes with entrance exam procedure and to master programmes with selection procedure. You can only apply for these degree programmes once a year (in spring)! So make sure you find out in good time whether your desired degree programme has an entrance exam/selection procedure.

## Step 5: Wait for your admission letter

VFS Global will send the results of your competence check directly to the University of Vienna.

Once you have submitted your application for admission to a degree programme, we will review your documents and the results of your competence check from VFS Global. This may take several weeks. We will send our admission letter to the e-mail address you have provided in u:space.
