# Welcome to the communities of the University of Vienna!

*Section: Study organisation · Language: en · Source: https://studieren.univie.ac.at/en/student-life-who-can-help-me-with/univie-communities/*

The communities at the University of Vienna offer you the ideal opportunity to try out new things and meet fascinating people at the same time. Here you find groups of students that make university life more colourful, diverse and exciting. Check it out and find your #univie community!
