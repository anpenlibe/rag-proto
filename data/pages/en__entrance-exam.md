## News for your start in the 2026/27 academic year

The tests for programmes with an entrance exam procedure take place from 6 July 2026 onwards. The application period is from 2 March to 4 May 2026.

## Your path to a study place in 2026/27

- ### Step 1

  **From now on**

  Find out more:

  - [What is an entrance exam/aptitude test procedure?](#c4427)
  - [Does my preferred programme have an entrance exam/aptitude test procedure?](#c7588)
  - [How do I find my dream degree programme?](#c4476)
  - [What will change in 2026/27?](https://aufnahmeverfahren.univie.ac.at/en/news/detail/entrance-exam-procedure-2026-27)
- ### Step 2

  **Deadline: 2 March to 4 May 2026**

  The application period has ended. There is no extension period. You can only take part in this year’s entrance exam/aptitude test procedure if you applied within the application period.

  Please note: The entrance exam and aptitude test procedure only take place once per academic year!
- ### Step 3

  **Written tests: From 6 July 2026 onwards**

  The written tests take place on site in Vienna. We will inform you until the end of this year about the exact test dates. There are no alternative dates. Therefore, make sure to keep the test day free!

  Note: The entrance exam and aptitude test procedures of the University of Vienna only take place once per academic year!
- ### Step 4

  **Results: Around 2 weeks after the test**

  We will send your test result via e-mail. If you were successful, you will receive info about your further steps for your admission and starting your studies.

  The winter semester always starts on 1 October.

## What does entrance exam and aptitude test procedure mean?

The University of Vienna conducts an entrance exam or aptitude test procedure for certain bachelor's and diploma programmes. This means: a written test will take place in July 2026.

On this page, you can find out everything you need to know about the programme content, deadlines, required documents and the test procedure. We provide a step by step guide on how to prepare and how to successfully apply.
